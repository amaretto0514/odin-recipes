Dish: Aji de gallina

Description

"Aji de gallina" is a dish from Peru. Is a classical dish from Perú and was created by mochicas.

Ingrediets

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 1 MAGGI Chicken Flavor Cube
  • 1 Can of IDEAL® CREMOSITA
  • 8 Slices of Sliced Bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Cups of Cooked Rice
  • 2 Cooked White Potatoes
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Teaspoon of Ground Garlic
  • 4 Tablespoons of Ground Yellow Chili Pepper
  • 4 Black Olives
  • 1 Teaspoon Chopstick
  • 1/4 Cup of Pecans
  • 2 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. In a pot, place the chicken with 1 liter of water. Boil for 30 minutes over medium heat. Reserve.
  2. Boil the eggs by placing them in a pot with water until they are covered. Cook them for 8 minutes from when the water boils. Peel.
  3. Cut the onion into cubes and the pecans into pieces. Place the chopped bread in a bowl and add 1/2 cup of IDEAL® CREMOSITA. Let it rest for 10 minutes until completely softened. Blend the bread and set aside.
  4. Heat the oil in a pot and fry the onion for 5 minutes. Blend and return the mixture to the pot.
  5. Add the garlic, yellow chili, and a MAGGI® Sabor Gallina cube to the pot and cook for 5 minutes over low heat. Add the blended bread and 2 cups of the chicken broth and stir slowly over low heat until the mixture boils and thickens slightly.
  6. Serve the chicken chili accompanied by 1/2 cup of rice, 1/2 white potato, 1/2 egg and chopped pecans. Decorate with an olive.
