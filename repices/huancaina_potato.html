Dish: Huancaina Potato

Description

Huancaina Potato is a typical starter of Peruvian gastronomy that has spread to diferent regions pf Peryu. It is on of the most popular and representative gastronomy of Peru

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • yellow chillis
  • soy sauce
  • boiled eggs
  • Dark Olives
  • letuce
  • Milk
  • vegetable Oli
  • Salt
  • Fresh Cheese
  • Soda Cockies

Steps

  1. Cut the chillis in pieces
  2. Put in the chillis with milk, coockies, cheese and salt on the blender
  3. Pour the vegetable oil into it until obten a dense and yellow sauce
  4. Boil the potatoes and peel
  5. cut in slices and add the sauce
  6. decore with the olives, letuce and eggs
  7. Enjoy.
